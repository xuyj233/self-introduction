## FinRipple — Event Ripple Awareness in Financial Markets
- **Collaborators:** Jianing Hao, Guang Zhang  
- **Summary:** Aligns large language models with financial market narratives to reason about cascading event impacts.  
- **Status:** Published at ACL 2025  

## LENS — Large Transformer for Financial Time Series Regularities
- **Collaborators:** Jianing Hao, Guang Zhang  
- **Summary:** Designs a pre-trained transformer to uncover temporal dependencies in noisy financial series.  
- **Status:** Published at ICAIF 2025  

## Hard Sample Aware Prompt-Tuning
- **Collaborators:** Qi An, Zaiqing Nie  
- **Summary:** Introduces difficulty-aware prompt adjustments to improve few-shot generalization in language models.  
- **Status:** Published at ACL 2023  

## Neural SDEs for Market Dynamics
- **Collaborators:** Jianing Hao, Guang Zhang  
- **Summary:** Explores state-aware and discretization-robust neural stochastic differential equation models for multi-modal market data.  
- **Status:** Under review at ICLR 2026  

## BizCompass — Reasoning Benchmark for Business LLMs
- **Collaborators:** Jianing Hao, Yuhe Wu, Guang Zhang  
- **Summary:** Builds an evaluation suite to measure LLM reasoning in business knowledge and decision-making tasks.  
- **Status:** In preparation for ACL Rolling Review, October 2025 cycle
